# pixljam
PixlJam 2017 hackathon @ Victoria Uni, Wellington


KIWI BOUNCE
Help the blue morepork take his struggling kiwi friend to the skies!
But beware of his appetite...

WASD to move, Space to jump
P to pick up your friend
O to let him go
